Upcoming Events

Find community events, public meetings, workshops, and festivals happening in Lesotho. Events are listed in date order. Want to add an event? Contact us here.

  • Cultural festivals and celebrations

April 2026

18 Apr

Riverside Development Public Consultation

📍 Civic Centre, Maseru  |  🕕 18:00 – 20:00  |  Free entry

The planning committee will present the updated Riverside District expansion proposal and take questions from the public. All Maseru residents are encouraged to attend and submit written comments during the 30-day consultation period.

22 Apr

Youth Media Workshop – Introduction to Community Reporting

📍 Maseru Public Library, Room 3  |  🕘 09:00 – 13:00  |  Ages 14–22, Free

Learn the basics of journalism — how to find a story, interview community members, and write a clear news article. Facilitated by the Community Media Lesotho team. Limited to 20 participants. Register via our contact form.

26 Apr

Free Health Screening Day

📍 St. Mary's Hall, Maseru  |  🕗 07:00 – 15:00  |  Free, all welcome

Free blood pressure, diabetes, and HIV testing offered by the Ministry of Health mobile clinic team. No appointment needed. Bring your national ID. Refreshments will be provided.

May 2026

02 May

Lesotho Heritage Festival 2026 – Opening Day

📍 City Park, Maseru  |  🕙 10:00 – 22:00  |  Adults M50, children under 12 free

The biggest cultural celebration of the year returns! Day one features opening ceremonies, live performances from 15 local artists, traditional food stalls, and the launch of a new youth stage. The festival runs 2–4 May.

10 May

Monthly Neighbourhood Cleanup Drive

📍 Meet at Main Road Park, Maseru  |  🕗 07:00 – 11:00  |  Volunteers welcome

Join your neighbours for the monthly cleanup. This month's focus areas are the Caledon River footpath and Maseru's market square. Wear comfortable clothes and bring water. Tea and snacks provided at the end.

June 2026

20 Jun

Digital Skills for Small Businesses

📍 Maseru Community Centre, Computer Lab  |  🕙 10:00 – 14:00  |  Free, bring a laptop

A practical workshop covering social media marketing, WhatsApp Business, and basic bookkeeping apps. Hosted by the Lesotho Small Business Development Forum. Register via our contact form.

How to Register for Events

  1. Find an event you are interested in above.
  2. Check whether registration is required (listed in the event details).
  3. If registration is required, go to our Contact page.
  4. Select "Submit an Event Registration" from the subject dropdown.
  5. Include the event name and your full name in the message.
  6. We will confirm your registration within 2 working days.
