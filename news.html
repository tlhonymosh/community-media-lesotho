Development

Maseru Riverside Project to Bring 500 New Jobs

By Sarah Mokoena – 14 March 2026

A proposed R2 billion mixed-use development in Maseru has divided community members. The Riverside District project would include shops, housing, and a community centre. While many welcome the estimated 500 new jobs, residents in affected areas fear they will be forced to relocate without proper compensation.

Education

Lesotho Schools Record 18% Literacy Improvement

By David Ntuli – 13 March 2026

Three pilot schools in Maseru District have reported an 18% improvement in reading scores after one term of the new literacy programme introduced by the Ministry of Education and Training. Teachers say the structured reading periods have made a noticeable difference in learner confidence.

Health

Mobile Clinics to Reach Rural Districts of Lesotho

By Lindiwe Dlamini – 12 March 2026

The Ministry of Health has launched three mobile clinics that will rotate through underserved areas including parts of Mohale's Hoek, Quthing, and Thaba-Tseka. Services will include maternal health checks, vaccinations, and TB screening.

Environment

200 Volunteers Clean the Caledon River

By Thabo Sithole – 11 March 2026

More than 200 volunteers from communities along the Caledon River joined the annual cleanup and removed 3 tonnes of waste. It was the largest turnout in the event's eight-year history. Organisers say pollution from plastic waste remains a serious concern for the river's health.

Heritage Festival Returns With Biggest Programme Yet

By Nomsa Khumalo – 10 March 2026

The Lesotho Heritage Festival returns in May 2026 with 40 local artists, traditional food stalls, and a new youth stage. Organisers say this year's three-day event in Maseru's City Park will be the biggest in the festival's ten-year history.

Council Approves M15 Million Sports Complex for Maseru

By Mpho Letsie – 9 March 2026

After two years of campaigning by local youth groups, the Maseru City Council has approved a M15 million budget for a new multi-sport community complex. Construction is expected to begin in the second half of 2026.
