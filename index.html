Welcome

Community Media Lesotho is a local news platform dedicated to covering stories that matter to people across the Kingdom of Lesotho. From the streets of Maseru to the villages of Leribe and Mohale's Hoek, we report on news, events, health, education, and culture.

Use the links above to read the latest news, check upcoming community events, watch video interviews with local leaders, or get in touch with our team.

What We Cover

  • Local government and community affairs
  • Education and schools in Lesotho
  • Health services and public clinics
  • Environment and conservation
  • Arts, culture, and heritage
  • Sports and youth development
