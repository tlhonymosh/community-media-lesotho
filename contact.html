What You Can Send Us

  • News tips about events happening in your community
  • Photos from local events with captions
  • Suggestions for people we should interview on video
  • Details of community events to add to our calendar
  • Letters and opinions about issues affecting your district

What Happens After You Contact Us

  1. Your message is received by our team in Maseru.
  2. We read every message within one working day.
  3. A team member will reply to you within 2 working days.
  4. If your story tip is accepted, a reporter will contact you to arrange an interview.
  5. If your event is approved, it will appear on our Events Calendar page.
