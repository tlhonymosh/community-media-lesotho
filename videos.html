Video Interviews

Conversations with people from communities across Lesotho

Watch Our Interviews

Our team travels across Lesotho to speak with community members, local leaders, teachers, health workers, and young people. Watch the interviews below.

Why We Do Video Interviews

  • To give community members a voice beyond written articles
  • To hear directly from local leaders and decision-makers
  • To document stories that might otherwise go unheard
  • To reach community members who prefer watching over reading

How to Suggest an Interview

  1. Think of a community member or leader whose story deserves to be heard.
  2. Go to our Contact Us page.
  3. Select "Video Interview Suggestion" from the subject dropdown.
  4. Write a short paragraph explaining who the person is and why their story matters.
  5. Include your own contact details so we can follow up with you.
  6. Our team will review the suggestion and be in touch within one week.
